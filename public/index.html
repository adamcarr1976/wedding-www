Directions

Suppliers

We have engaged with local suppliers for all of our services, this means we are actively contributing to the local economy and helping businesses within East Anglia and surrounding area.

Isaacs on the Quay

Isaacs is more than a pub, they serve on average over 100 plates of great food a day, host over 80 weddings a year and have over 40 private parties every year too.

Love Mee Designs

Hannah designed our amazing infinity logo as well as designing and making all of our invites. She is also helping with a design for our favours and has taken my rough ideas and turned them into beautiful designs. She is friendly and professional and we really cannot recommend her enough!

Photography by Emily Fae

Emily is amazing! From the moment we met we knew we wanted her to photograph our wedding. She is friendly, fun and bubbly. We knew she would fit perfectly into our day and we would have photos that would capture the joy, love and memories forever. Emily brings creativity to all that she does and loves making sure life is full of fun and colour.

The Event DJ's

Luke is the resident DJ at Isaacs so it seemed like a perfect fit for us. They use the highest quality and latest equipment for all their events, they get it right and won't let you down.

Henley Flowers

Henley Flowers is an independent floral design company based in the village of Henley. Bev provides top quality flowers for any occasion and really listens to what you want and need to make the perfect arrangements.

Amazing Cakes by Jean

Amazing cakes describes Jean's creations perfectly. After trying a sample at a wedding fair we were blown away by the flavour and texture.

Oh Sew Vintage

An amazing bespoke dressmaking service based in Hertfordshire, yes I know it's outside of East Anglia but they are that good! They have created Kirstys lovely wedding dress and Evie's flower girl dress as well.

Coes

Coes of Ipswich offer the most extensive range of men’s and boy’s formal wear hire in the region. They have an excellent reputation for delivering great quality suits and they were they only company we considered to supply the suits for the groom, the best man and both of our dads.

Riley & Riley

Kirsty's ring came from Riley & Riley. They are friendly and professional, offer excellent service and provide great quality jewellery.
